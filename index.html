Meet the collection of figures
by pop artist KAWS.

SIGNATURE

Alphabet "X"

Kaws' work may be characterized
by repeated use of "x"
in the hands, nose, eyes, and ears,
and a focus on colors, lines, and unique graphics,
such as the exclusive use of pop culture icons.

Parody

While considering ways to approach people
in a familiar way,
Kaws found that popular characters
like Mickey Mouse, Simpson, and SpongeBob
are loved by everyone regardless of
language or culture.
So Kaws' work began with a famous character motif.

Brian Donnelly

Andy Warhol in the 21st century.

This is what people call Brian Donnelly,
a pop artist and designer from New York,
the United States known as Cowes.

The most famous one is the character 'Company',
which made 'X' his symbol.

HYPEBEAST: KAWS COMPANION

Kaws Companion is a collection of characters
by Kanye West, Travis Scott and Drake.

From 100-foot sculptures to 11-inch collections,
you can see them in museums around the world.

HYPEBEAST explains why Kaws' iconic Companion character
has been resold for thousands of dollars
and has become today's Mickey Mouse version.

CAREER

1996s

Working as a freelance animator
at Disney.
Background work such as
'101 Dalmatians', 'Daria, and Doug'.

Late 1990s

Design and production of
limited vinyl toys.
He started drawing attention
by creating a character (COMPANION)
with his own unique design.

The early 1990s

Working as a graffiti artist.
He spread the sub-tying culture
by mixing his designs with bus stops
or public phones.

The early 2000s

Collaborate with familiar cartoon characters.
Collaborated with 'The Simpsons', 'The Mickey Mouse',
'The Michelin', 'The Smurfs', and 'SpongeBob'.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Can I buy a limited edition product?

Yes, it's possible.

In the case of limited edition products,
the pre-sale schedule is shared through the notice
after the product is secured.

If you want to be notified of the pre-sale schedule,
you can log in and set the notification
in the Notification Settings on your My Page.

How long does it take to deliver?

The delivery period varies depending on
the type of product you purchased.

In general, it takes 2–5 days for domestic shipping
and 2–3 weeks for overseas shipping,
but the delivery period may vary depending on
various variables such as custom-made and
temporarily out of stock.

* Please note that the information period is the expected time
and there is a possibility of change depending on
the company's situation.

Can I cancel my purchase?

Yes, it's possible.

1. After logging in as a member/non-member,
enter your order number and email address
in My Page - Order / Return.

2. Please select or write
the product you want to cancel
and the reason for cancellation.

3. You will receive a confirmation email
when your order has been cancelled.

* However, if the order has already been received
and is ready for delivery,
it is not possible to cancel the order.

The product was damaged during delivery.

If the delivered product is damaged,
please contact us via email or SNS account
and we will contact you separately after contacting
the courier.

The reply may take 1-2 days.
