Brian Donnelly

Andy Warhol in the 21st century.

a pop artist and designer from New York,
the United States known as Cowes.

HYPEBEAST: KAWS COMPANION

Kaws Companion is a collection of characters
by Kanye West, Travis Scott and Drake.

From 100-foot sculptures to 11-inch collections,
you can see them in museums around the world.

HYPEBEAST explains why Kaws' iconic Companion character
has been resold for thousands of dollars
and has become today's Mickey Mouse version.

CAREER

1996s

Working as a freelance animator at Disney.
Background work such as '101 Dalmatians', 'Daria, and Doug'.

Late 1990s

Design and production of limited vinyl toys.
He started drawing attention
by creating a character (COMPANION)
with his own unique design.

The early 1990s

Working as a graffiti artist.
He spread the sub-tying culture
by mixing his designs with bus stops
or public phones.

The early 2000s

Collaborate with familiar cartoon characters.
Collaborated with 'The Simpsons', 'The Mickey Mouse',
'The Michelin', 'The Smurfs', 'and SpongeBob'.
